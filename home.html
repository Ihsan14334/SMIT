Shaheen Afridi

( Pashto: شاہین شاہ آفریدی; born 6 April 2000) is a Pakistani international cricketer who plays for the Pakistan cricket team across all formats. He is widely regarded as one of the best bowlers in the world. He captains the Lahore Qalandars in the PSL and under his leadership, Lahore Qalandars won their first PSL title in the 2022 season and again went on to win the 2023 season, becoming the first team in PSL history to successfully defend their title. He is the first Pakistani to win the Garfield Sobers trophy.
